Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Kodoshin is committed to protecting your personal data and giving you full control over your GitHub information.

1. Information We Collect

  • GitHub username and public profile information.
  • Selected repositories (only those you grant access to).
  • Basic usage statistics (e.g. usage frequency, performance).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Personalize your experience and assistant recommendations.
  • Provide insights and solutions relevant to your codebase.
  • Improve product performance and functionality.

3. Your Control

You can revoke access at any time via GitHub. No data is stored or shared outside your session unless required for service delivery.

4. Security

We use encryption and industry best practices to protect your data. Your code remains private unless explicitly shared.

5. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at contact@kodoshin.com.
